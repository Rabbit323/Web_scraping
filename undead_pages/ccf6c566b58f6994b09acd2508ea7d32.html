Remodel Tips

    • Have a realistic budget in mind
    • Print or draw out your ideas
    • Utilize sites such as Houzz & Pinterest for ideas

    • Permit Facts

    • Permits must be pulled for most Remodel Projects
    • Almost all permits will require a fee
    • Permit process time is anywhere from 1- 6 weeks

    • “Apples to Apples”

    Please make sure when comparing quotes from different contractors that all materials and services included are identical.
