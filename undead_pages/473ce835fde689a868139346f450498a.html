National Skill Standards

OP/OSS

An Occupational Profile/Occupational Skill Standard is written specification of the practical skills, underlying knowledge, and experience demonstrated by an individual in a particular occupation. The development of the Occupational Profile (OP)/Occupational Skill Standard (OSS) involves thorough analysis of the existing OP/OSS in Technical Sub-Committee Meeting and it is followed by Two Days DACUM workshop, One day Task Verification Workshop. The finalized OP/OSS is then recommended to NSTB for approval.
